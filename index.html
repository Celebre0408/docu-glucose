Project Documentation

Introduction



Glucose and sugar were actually developed because of my mother. Initially, the system was called an e-notebook, which is an online e-notebook, but then my mother was hospitalized and needed to monitor her water intake and blood sugar. I decided to revise the interface and functions according to her needs.


Scope and Limitation



The project scope is not that broad; it has 2 interfaces, the water and the sugar. The water shows the water intake for the past 7 days, and it's the same in the sugar interface, but the difference is the sugar has 2 graphs, the AM and PM, or day and night, for those patients that need to monitor their blood sugar twice a day. Additionally, both will turn red if you exceed the limit; for example, in water, it will turn red if you consume more than 1000 cc, and in sugar, it will turn red if it exceeds 120 in the morning and 130 at night. The project is cross-platform, as it's working across different devices. It's actually web-based, but we have an Android APK using median.co. The limitation of the project as of now is you can't print reports due to hosting problems. In InfinityFree you can upload up to 10 files per upload, but you can't upload the vendor, as it has 100+ files inside it.

About Project Development



During the development I used Visual Studio Code for editing and coding, HTML for structure, Tailwind CSS and Daisy UI for the design, JavaScript for tables, PHP for the server-side language, and MySQL for the database. About hosting. I use InfinityFree for free hosting, but 5 GB is the reason why I disabled profile pictures in the profile section.
